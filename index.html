Competition Format

Kreativity League will be in 2 Stages, 4 rounds.

Stage 1: Preparation Stage
  • Round 1: Unleash the Hidden Skills.
  • Round 2: Mentoring to Nurture Problem-Solving Skills.
Stage 2: Competition Stage
  • Round 3: Problem-Solving Challenge at Zonal level.
  • Round 4: Zonal winners will compete at the National Level.

Procedure

Open for students of Ages 8-17 years

Age categories for Round 1 : 8-9 years, 10-11 years, 12-13 years, 14-15 years and 16-7 years.

Age categories for Round 2,3 & 4: 8-10 years, 11-14 years, 15-17 years.

In Stage 2 competitions Students can compete individually or in a team of a maximum of 3 in their respective age group.

First 3 rounds will be in Online mode and the final round will be conducted in IIT Delhi.

The League consists of multiple rounds, including MCQ Quiz, Mentoring, and problem-solving challenges.

The medium of instruction will be in English.

Infrastructure Required

A computing device (PC,Laptop,Tablet,Phone)

Web Camera

A stable internet connection (Wifi,Mobile Data)

Internet Browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)


Round 4 will be conducted offline in IIT Delhi.
