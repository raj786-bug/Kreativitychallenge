Competition Format

Kreativity League is in 2 Stages, 4 rounds.

S1: Preparation Stage
  • R1: Unleash the Hidden Skills. [for 8-17 years divided into 5 age groups]
  • R2: Mentoring to Nurture Problem-Solving Skills with Creativity. [Mentoring will be done in 3 age groups]
S2: Competition Stage
  • R1: Problem-Solving Challenge at Zonal level. [5 Zones in 3 age groups]
  • R2: Zonal winners will compete at the National Level in IIT Delhi. [Separate for 3 age groups]

Procedure

Open for students of Ages 8-17 years [Grade 3 to Grade 12].

In Stage 2 competitions Students can compete individually or in a team of a maximum of 3 in their respective age group.

First 3 rounds will be in Online mode and the final round will be conducted in IIT Delhi.

The League consists of multiple rounds, including MCQ Quiz, Mentoring, and problem-solving challenges.

The medium of instruction will be in English.
